About Us


Fogg perfumes are known for their long-lasting fragrances and are offered in a variety of scent profiles, appealing to both men and women according to Flipkart. Fogg was launched in 2011 by Vini Cosmetics. The brand's core offering is a no-gas body spray, guaranteeing numerous sprays per bottle according to Vini Cosmetics. Fogg has expanded its product line to include perfumes, making it a popular choice for both deodorants and perfumes.
